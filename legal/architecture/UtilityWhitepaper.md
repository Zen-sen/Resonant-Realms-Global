# ARCHITECTURAL WHITE-PAPER DISCLOSURE FOR THE RESONANT REALMS SAGA

## 1. ABSOLUTE UTILITY OBJECTIVE & NON-INVESTMENT ARCHITECTURE
The Resonant Realms Saga is engineered solely as an interactive, culture-focused digital match-3 puzzle game. All mechanics, lineage variables, and ancestral points are functional in-game configurations designed to reward strategic positioning, logical problem-solving, and structural engagement. 

The Platform does not issue, manage, or market investment items, financial derivatives, interest-yielding tokens, or security assets. No asset within this application represents a share, stake, debt obligation, or voting right in any corporate financial structure.

## 2. EXCLUSION OF EXPECTATION OF PROFIT
The lineage structures, including Avatar #0 and the 11 Cradle designations, carry absolutely no right to passive payouts, capital appreciation, structural financial profit, or liquid investment returns. Any external valuation assigned to secondary software interfaces is purely driven by external peer-to-peer interest. 

The application must not be utilized as a speculative tool, investment vehicle, or wealth preservation mechanism. Marketing resources focus strictly on cultural educational narratives, performance mechanics, and interactive puzzles.

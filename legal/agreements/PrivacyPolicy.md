# PRIVACY POLICY & TELEMETRY PROCESSING ADDENDUM

## 1. SPECIALIZED BIOMETRIC TELEMETRY AND SPEED VECTOR MONITORING
To ensure game safety and block malicious automated software bots, our serverless edge validation engines ingest, log, and analyze real-time player telemetry data. 

When you play the Match-3 engine, the Platform collects:
- Device coordinate touches and click-vector coordinates.
- Precise millisecond timing differences between successive interface modifications.
- Swipe velocities and gesture dimensions.

This processing is strictly conducted to ensure compliance with our security benchmarks under Section 11 of the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR (Legitimate Interests). These behavioral metrics are explicitly used for fraud prevention and to verify your identity as a real human user.

## 2. PERSISTENT LOCAL ENVIRONMENT CACHING
The Platform uses standard local storage parameters (`localStorage`) within the browser application loop to cache user session profiles, cryptographic validation tokens, and authentication states across successive system updates. This data remains on your local machine and can be cleared via your local browser utility settings.

## 3. IMMUTABLE DECENTRALIZED DATA RETENTION RULES
Under local data laws (POPIA), users generally retain rights to request information removal or record corrections. However, you explicitly recognize that transactions executing calls to our core Diamond architecture write state modifications permanently to the underlying public blockchain ledger. 

Public key identifiers, transaction records, and on-chain lineage bindings cannot be modified, hidden, or deleted. Engaging with the contract layer represents your explicit approval to publish your public wallet address across the immutable blockchain landscape.

# DIRECTIVE 11 ELECTRONIC RISK AND COMPLIANCE RETURN (RCR) ARCHITECTURE

## 1. COMPLIANCE FILING COMPILATION INDEX
As required by Directive 11 of the Financial Intelligence Centre (FIC), the platform maintains real-time data collection channels to easily generate mandatory electronic Risk and Compliance Returns (RCR). These automated data profiles are organized to streamline your scheduled reporting requirements.

## 2. THREE-YEAR HISTORICAL LEDGER TRACKING SPECIFICATIONS
The application platform preserves all operational transaction records for a rolling history of three (3) years. This historical database tracks:
- Total unique public ledger addresses interacting with our Diamond proxy contract layer.
- Consolidated transaction values processed through our validation endpoints.
- Geographic system metrics flagged by client IP routing points.

This data is securely stored to protect user privacy while ensuring the platform can easily fulfill official information requests or regulatory compliance checks from domestic authorities.

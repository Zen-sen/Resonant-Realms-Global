# SECTION 42 FICA RISK MANAGEMENT AND COMPLIANCE PROGRAMME (RMCP)

## 1. REGULATORY CLASSIFICATION CONTEXT
In accordance with amendments to the Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA) of South Africa, entities dealing in Crypto Assets are classified under Item 22 of Schedule 1 as Accountable Institutions. 

The Resonant Realms Saga operates primarily as a utility gaming matrix. It does not provide third-party brokerage, custody services, automated exchange clearing pools, or fiat-to-crypto gateway systems. As a result, its transactional footprint carries a low inherent systemic financial crime risk.

## 2. THE ZERO-THRESHOLD EVENT LOGGING PROTOCOL
To maintain compliance with domestic transaction tracing frameworks, the application logs all underlying smart contract invocations and associated public wallet addresses. Because South African AML oversight has no minimal transaction value threshold (zero de minimis floor) for digital asset monitoring, the platform records all interactions with our smart contract layer to maintain clean compliance records.

## 3. UNHOSTED WALLET DATA LOGGING PARAMETERS
All interactions on the Platform route via decentralized, non-custodial (unhosted) user wallets through the Pi Network SDK wrapper. The application records transaction hashes, connecting public ledger IDs with specific match-3 server signatures to build complete audit trails for internal review.
